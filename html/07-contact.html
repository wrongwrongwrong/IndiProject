Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How does the custom description service work?

Our AI-powered system analyzes your server details and generates professional descriptions tailored to your game type and style preferences. You can request revisions until you're satisfied.

Is my payment information secure?

Yes, we use enterprise-grade encryption and follow PCI DSS compliance standards. All payment processing is handled by secure, certified payment processors.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Absolutely! You can cancel your subscription at any time from your account dashboard. You'll continue to have access to paid features until the end of your billing period.

Do you offer custom solutions for large gaming communities?

Yes! Our Enterprise plan includes custom integrations, white-label solutions, and dedicated support. Contact our sales team to discuss your specific needs.

How quickly can I get started?

You can create your first custom description within minutes of signing up. Our platform is designed for immediate use with no complex setup required.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and cryptocurrency payments. Enterprise customers can also arrange for invoicing and bank transfers.
